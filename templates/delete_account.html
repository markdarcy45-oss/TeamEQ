Delete Your Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What will be deleted:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • All player data you've created
  • All match results and statistics
  • League memberships
  • All associated game data

How to request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@teameq.app (or your actual email)
  2. Include your username in the email
  3. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  4. Your request will be processed within 48 hours

Alternative: If you only want to remove yourself from a specific league, contact your league administrator instead of deleting your entire account.
